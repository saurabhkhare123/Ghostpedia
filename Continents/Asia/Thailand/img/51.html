Krahang
Krahang is a male spirit of the Thai folklore. It manifests itself as a shirtless man, wearing a traditional loincloth, who flies in the night. Krahang uses two large kradong, round rice winnowing baskets, to fly in the night in rural areas of Thailand. It also often rides a sak tam khao, the long wooden pestle of a traditional manual rice pounder.WikipediaMore at Wikipedia
